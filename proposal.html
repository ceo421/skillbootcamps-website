Executive Summary

SWCyber Nexus CIC is revolutionizing IT careers by transforming non-technical professionals into high-earning specialists through comprehensive Skills Bootcamps. Our bootcamps specifically target the critical skills shortage in AI, automation, and digital transformation - turning career changers, women returning to work, and neurodiverse learners into £30K-£70K earning IT professionals without requiring coding backgrounds. Our innovative Learning Management System (LMS) platform ensures 100% online delivery with accessibility-first design, making high-paying IT careers accessible to all Somerset residents.

1. Organization Overview

SWCyber Nexus CIC is a community interest company dedicated to democratizing AI education and creating inclusive learning environments. We specialize in supporting underrepresented groups in technology, with particular focus on:

  • Career changers transitioning to AI/digital roles
  • Women returning to work after career breaks
  • Neurodiverse learners and those with learning difficulties
  • Adults upskilling to stay relevant in the AI economy

2. Proposed Structured Qualification Progression

Our Skills Bootcamps (2026) and Apprenticeships (2027) are designed as a progressive pathway from Level 2 to Level 5, enabling learners to build their IT careers step by step without requiring technical backgrounds:

2.1 Level 2 Foundation Bootcamps

Digital Literacy Skills With AI Bootcamp

Duration: 12 weeks | Level: 2 | Target: Neurodiverse learners and adults entering digital environments for the first time

  • Foundations of Digital Confidence: Intro to devices, OS, internet safety, navigating files
  • Communication & Collaboration: Email, calendar, video meetings, collaborative docs
  • AI-Powered Everyday Tools: ChatGPT, AI summaries, visual AI (Canva, DALL•E), voice assistants
  • Workplace Tech Readiness: Cloud drives, task managers, calendars, forms & surveys
  • Responsible AI Use: Understanding AI bias, data privacy, accessibility
  • Accessible Tools for Neurodiverse Users: Screen readers, dictation tools, visual planners, task boards
  • No prerequisites required - perfect starting point for future bootcamps

AI Productivity Specialist Bootcamp

Duration: 12 weeks | Level: 2 | Target: Women returning to work, admin professionals, and career changers looking to transition into hybrid digital roles

  • Foundations of AI & Automation: What AI is, how it works, business impact
  • Personal & Team Productivity: Time management, task automation, planning with AI
  • AI for Communication & Documentation: Emails, reports, meeting notes, chat summaries
  • AI in Admin & Operations: Calendar AI, CRM tools, budgeting sheets with AI
  • Data & Insight Tools: AI dashboards, Excel with Copilot, Google Looker Studio
  • Building AI Workflows: Using tools like Zapier, Make.com, Notion AI
  • Career outcomes: AI Productivity Specialist (£28K-£40K), Business Automation Consultant (£35K-£50K)

2.2 Level 3 Intermediate Bootcamps

Vibe Coding, AI Agents & Automation Developer Bootcamp

Duration: 12 weeks | Level: 3 | Prerequisite: Level 2 AI Productivity Specialist

  • Coding Foundations: HTML, CSS, JavaScript basics, Python syntax, functions, and logic, GitHub and version control
  • Coding with AI: Using ChatGPT to learn and debug, GitHub Copilot for coding, Prompt engineering basics
  • APIs & AI Integrations: Working with OpenAI and Google APIs, Fetching data from APIs, Securing and testing APIs
  • Web Apps & AI Interfaces: Build basic web apps (JS/Python/Streamlit), Streamlit for AI tools, Frontend UX principles
  • Building AI Agents: Intro to LangChain & LLM orchestration, Memory & tools in AI agents, Chain of thought, ReAct, BabyAGI frameworks
  • No-Code AI & Automation: Zapier, Make.com, Voiceflow, Building AI workflows & assistants, Connecting tools with webhooks
  • Accessibility, Ethics & Inclusive Dev: Inclusive UX for neurodiverse users, WCAG accessibility standards, Testing for fairness and privacy
  • Capstone & Portfolio: Building your AI agent project, Testing, feedback, refinement, Deploy and present
  • Career outcomes: Junior AI Automation Developer, AI Solutions Assistant, Prompt Engineer, No-Code Developer, Technical Operations Specialist, Citizen Developer

AI Ethics & Innovation Officer Bootcamp

Duration: 12 weeks | Level: 3 | Prerequisite: Level 2 AI Productivity Specialist

  • Understanding AI in Society & Work: What AI is, types, capabilities, and real-world use cases, AI in society: impact on jobs, economy, and inclusion
  • Frameworks for Ethical AI: Ethical principles (fairness, accountability, transparency, safety, privacy), Responsible AI frameworks: OECD, UNESCO, UK Government AI Guidance
  • Law, Regulation & Compliance: Overview of AI regulation in the UK, EU AI Act, and global laws, GDPR and data privacy principles related to AI
  • Tools for Governance & Auditing: AI audit tools: what they are and why they matter, Interpreting model outputs (e.g. bias detection), Tools overview: IBM AI Fairness 360, Google What-If, Fiddler
  • Strategic Innovation with Responsible AI: What is innovation strategy in the age of AI?, Managing responsible AI pilots and cross-functional teams
  • Leadership, Communication & Career Pathways: Presenting AI ethics and innovation proposals, Communicating risk and opportunity to stakeholders
  • Career outcomes: AI Governance Assistant, Responsible AI Coordinator, Digital Policy & Ethics Officer, AI Risk & Innovation Consultant

AI For Career Changers Bootcamp

Duration: 16 weeks | Level: 3 | Target: Technical career transition

  • Python programming and machine learning fundamentals
  • AI development tools and portfolio development
  • Data analysis and technical project management
  • Career outcomes: AI Analyst, Data Scientist, Technical Consultant
