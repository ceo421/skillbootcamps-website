About SkillMindLab

A Community Interest Company dedicated to making AI education accessible to everyone, with a focus on career changers and underrepresented groups.

Our Mission

SWCyber Nexus CIC is proud to propose as a Skills Bootcamps training provider, delivering UK Government-backed programs in AI and Digital Transformation through our revolutionary, cutting-edge LMS platform. Our advanced learning management system combines state-of-the-art technology with personalized learning pathways, real-time progress tracking, and immersive interactive content.

Our groundbreaking platform features AI-powered adaptive learning, comprehensive career guidance tools, integrated assessment systems, and seamless collaboration features. Built with accessibility and trauma-informed design at its core, our LMS ensures every learner, regardless of their background or learning style, can achieve their career transformation goals.

Our founder brings 15+ years of teaching experience and personal understanding of career transition challenges, neurodiversity, and accessibility needs, ensuring our platform delivers exceptional learning experiences that prepare learners for real-world career success.
